Overview

Purpose

Our purpose is to provide a fun, wild and wet time for everyone in a safe yet still entertaining manner. We also accomplish this with no hassle to the patron, as we provide the wares and location beforehand, all in the name of ease of access to the wonders of white water rafting.

Audience

We provide rafting support for all skill levels and backgrounds. Are you new to the world of rafting? We will walk you through the process and show you more beginner friendly areas! Are you a veteren to rafting? We will provide what we can and recommend some more advanced areas for rafting! Are you a solo rider looking for a group to go with? We can locate and help you find people in your area to raft with!
